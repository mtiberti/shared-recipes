Pancake Recipe

Ingredients
1 1/2 cup flour
2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 salt
1 cup of milk
1 egg
1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions
Step 1
In a large bowl mix dry ingredients (Flour, baking powder and soda,and salt)
Step 2 
Add milk egg and oil
Step 3
Heat a skillet and poor 1/4 cup pancake batter
Step 4 
Let cook until air bubbles are present on the pancake and flip pancake
Step 5
Add any toping including fresh fruit strawberries bannanas and maple syrup
